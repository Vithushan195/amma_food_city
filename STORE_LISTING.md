## Full Description (4000 chars)

Amma Food City brings the best of South Asian groceries straight to your doorstep. From authentic Sri Lankan spices and Tamil staples to fresh vegetables and ready-to-cook ingredients — everything you need for your kitchen, delivered fast.

WHY CHOOSE AMMA FOOD CITY?

🥭 Authentic Products
Browse hundreds of genuine South Asian products including Basmati rice, curry leaves, coconut milk, spice blends, lentils, chutneys, snacks, and much more. All sourced from trusted suppliers.

🚚 Fast Delivery
Get your groceries delivered in 30-45 minutes. Choose your preferred 2-hour delivery slot from 9AM to 9PM, 7 days a week. Free delivery on orders over £30.

💰 Great Value
Enjoy competitive prices, weekly special offers, and exclusive promo codes. Use AMMA10 for 10% off your first order!

🔒 Secure Payments
Pay securely with credit/debit card via Stripe, or choose Cash on Delivery. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.

📱 Easy to Use
Simple, beautiful interface designed for quick shopping. Search products, browse by category, save your favourite addresses, and reorder past orders in one tap.

FEATURES:
• Browse by category — Rice & Grains, Spices, Vegetables, Snacks, Beverages, and more
• Smart search with instant results
• Detailed product pages with nutrition info and weight variants
• Real-time order tracking from kitchen to doorstep
• Saved addresses for quick checkout
• Order history with easy reorder
• Promo codes and special offers
• Push notifications for order updates
